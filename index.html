FastCI

Software for IC50 and Drug Combination Analysis


Simple Data Import

Ever struggled with manual data entry? FastCI makes it easy to import raw data from your experiments, accepting Microsoft Excel files without any required pre-formatting.

No Pre-Processing Required

Data values can be blanked, normalized, and labeled in FastCI. Simply, highlight the appropriate cells and select the type, treatment, and concentration. Concentrations can be entered either individually or as serial dilutions.

Intuitive Design

As you apply labels, they are added directly to the cells. Cells are also color-coded based on treatment and concentration for easy visualization.

Easy Data Analysis and Export

Data are blanked and normalized before being fit with a sigmoidal curve to determine the IC50 values. For combination studies, the Chou-Talalay method is used to generate an Fa-CI plot and provide interpretations of synergy and antagonism.

Analyses can be saved in a FastCI-compatible format or exported for use in Graphpad Prism.
